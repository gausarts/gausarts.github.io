Permissions

The DruPocket offers features such as adding and deleting pictures or videos, storing files at external removable storage.

These features need your permissions. Without these permissions the app is crippled:

  • INTERNET to download pictures, or videos. The system may likely grant a little portion of this permission. This limited internet access may suffice. If however things do not function properly, be sure to enable the needed permissions.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE/ WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE to store photos and videos on your external SD card. None of your personal data is read or written/ overwritten.
  • MEDIA_CONTENT_CONTROL to play and stop the videos.
  • SET_WALLPAPER to set pictures or video covers as wallpapers.
  • CAMERA to add your own camera pictures into gallery.

They are only enabled on your own conscious approval. You will be asked for permissions before using this app. Also check out the Settings > Permissions to enable the rest. Again all is disabled by default until you enable it. If disallowed, they simply won't function. No hidden gimmicks.
