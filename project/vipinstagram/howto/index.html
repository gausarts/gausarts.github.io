Howto

Instagram
  1. Long press on any small thumbnails (videos or images) at listing pages: /tags, /explore, /profile, or search pages, etc.
  2. Or tap the BOOKMARK icon at listings with larger images, or full page views.
  3. Or tap the LIKE icon at listings with larger images, or full page views.
  4. Or long press the post creation TIME at listings with larger images, or full page views.
  5. Or tap the three vertical dots at the top right coder, and hit Copy link.

The first four are applicable if using the provided private/ internal web app. The last can be any public browsers, or Instagram app.

Twitter
  1. Video:
    • Long press on post creation TIME (e.g.: 1h or 19m) to the right of username at each card of listing pages.
    • Or tap the SHARE icon at bottom right of card at listings, and hit Copy link to Tweet.
  2. Image:
    • Long press on images internal to or hosted in Twitter. Not those leading to external sites, identified by https://t.co/... url.

Be informed! Only works for a stand-alone tweet, not the one embedded inside another tweet. Just visit its original tweet to download.

Tips!
Basically long tap on any links leading to the full pages if at listing page identified by small thumbnails. Or find and hit Copy link. If the images in this tutorial section are not visible, be sure to connect internet.
