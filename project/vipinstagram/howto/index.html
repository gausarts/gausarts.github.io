Howto

Instagram
Internal web app interface:
  1. Long press on any small thumbnails (videos or images) at listing pages: /tags, /explore, /profile, or search pages, etc.
  2. Or tap the BOOKMARK icon.
  3. Or tap the LIKE icon.
  4. Or long press the post creation TIME.
External browsers, or Instagram app:
  1. Tap the three vertical dots at the top right corner, and hit Copy link.

Be informed! The last option requires you provide credentials via Settings > Private mode. Unlike the internal, the external may trigger annoying New login email warning due to being less persistent sessions. Please use the internal for convenience regardless its limitations -- due to being web app, not native app. The same principle applies to other supported sites.

Twitter
  1. Video:
    • Long press on post creation TIME (e.g.: 1h or 19m) to the right of username at each card of listing pages.
    • Or tap the SHARE icon at bottom right of card listings, and hit Copy link to Tweet.
  2. Image:
    • Long press on images internal to or hosted in Twitter.

Be informed! Only works for a stand-alone tweet, not the one embedded inside another tweet, identified by a bordered box/ card. Just visit its original tweet to download. Images leading to external sites, identified by https://t.co/... url, not internally hosted at Twitter, are not supported.

TikTok/ Likee/ SnackVideo
These similar apps can follow the same procedures, except for SnackVideo which can only be downloaded via their Copy link.
  1. Video:
    • Long press on any small video thumbnails at profile pages.
    • Or tap the SHARE icon at bottom right, and hit Copy link.
  2. Image:
    • No supports.

Be informed! The web interface lacks of search and listing features. You can however search usernames using the internal search provided by this app at the top right corner. You can also download videos directly from TikTok app or its similar apps. The only differences are slightly smaller size, and integrated organization with other video providers if using this app. And no watermak for Like. For SnackVideo, please use the Copy link via their apps as it has no usable website.
