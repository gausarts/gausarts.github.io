Instagram
A. Via internal web app interface: This is a very minimal built-in browser. Everything is done in one step. Choose one, not all.
  1. Long press on any small thumbnails (videos or images) at listing pages: /tags, /explore, /profile, or search pages, etc.
  2. Or tap the BOOKMARK icon.
  3. Or tap the LIKE icon.
  4. Or long press the post creation TIME.
B. Via external browsers, or Instagram app: May take two or three steps. Tap the three vertical dots at the top right corner.
  1. Hit Copy link.
  2. Or hit Share link, and find VIP Downloader icon.

Be informed! Option B and scraping may require you providing dummy credentials via Settings > Accounts due to restrictions. Option B may trigger annoying New login email warnings due to being less persistent sessions. Please use the internal (option A) for convenience regardless its limitations -- due to being web app, not native app. The same principle likely applies to other supported sites, unless otherwise stated. Scraping requires Option A.

Twitter
  1. Video/ image:
    • Long press on post creation TIME (e.g.: 1h or 19m) to the right of username at each card of listing pages.
    • Or tap the SHARE icon at bottom right of card listings, and hit Copy link to Tweet.
  2. Image:
    • Long press on images internal to or hosted in Twitter. If many images, just long press on one as a representative for the entire images in the post, not all.

Be informed! Unlike plain images, or other video sites, long pressing on Twitter video image previews will not work. Only works for a stand-alone tweet, not the one embedded inside another tweet, identified by a bordered box/ card. Just visit its original tweet to download. Images leading to external sites, identified by https://t.co/... url, not internally hosted at Twitter, are not supported, yet.
